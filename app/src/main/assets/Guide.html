MoodMate - User Guide

Welcome to My App! This guide will help you get started and learn how to use the app.

Inputting Today's Mood Home Screen Mood Details Sharing Moods Well-being Notifications

Inputting Today's Mood

To start, you can enter your mood by inputting the following details on the main page:

  • Today's Mood Rating (5-1)
  • Date of Mood (DD-MM-YYYY)
  • Notes

Home Screen

Each mood is displayed on the Home Screen, showing the date and rating for that day.

Mood Details

Clicking on a specific mood on the Home Screen opens up a detail page displaying the date and any notes attached to that mood.

Sharing Moods

Clicking on the Share button on the Mood Details Screen allows the user to share their mood on a range of other apps.

Well-being

The Well-being screen displays different ways the user can improve their well-being. Suggestions are shown in a random order.

Notifications

A reminder notification is sent out every day at 8 PM to prompt the user to input their mood for the day.
